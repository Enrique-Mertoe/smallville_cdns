1. Yesu oluongowa mondo watine, Waland injili e piny, Dhumbe gi ogendini onego giyudie ler mar Ruoth Nyasaye.
    Injili mar hera, injili mar ng 'uono, injili en wach Nyasaye! x2
  2. Joote, jonabi nolando injili, e pinje duto te. Dhier, gi kuyo, sand gi tuech, masiche noyudogi.
  3. Mudho mar pinyni ogen'go injili, To Roho otelonwa; E pinje gi gode, e thimbe gi nembe yaluru injili ne ji.
  4. Yesu ochiko wadhiyo e pinje puonjo ji wach Nyasaye, Jogo moseyie wakelo ni Jawar Giyud lokruok mar chuny.
  5. Yesu koduogo mar omo nyithinde Moseko tiyone, Osimbo mar ngima, osimbo mar locho to Yesu oruakonwa.
